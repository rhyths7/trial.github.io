About Us

Founded on decades of expertise and trust, DRSS India stands as a leading name in the field of weighing, calibration, and cast iron weight manufacturing. With roots tracing back to 2001, we take pride in delivering precision, quality, and reliability across all our products and services. From supplying standard and customized cast iron weights to providing expert calibration and rental solutions, we continue to uphold the legacy of excellence that defines our brand.

Our Legacy

The foundation of DRSS India was built through the vision and dedication of two pioneering ventures : Sandeep Enterprises and SR International.

Sandeep Enterprises, established by Mr. Sandeep Deo in 2001, specialized in weighing machines, weighbridges, and calibration services, along with electrical products that meet industrial standards. The firm became a trusted name across industries for its Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMCs) and partnership with reputed brands like Hensel,ATCO,Sansui.

SR International, led by Mrs. Snehal Deo, focused on software components, digital integration, and service management, strengthening the technological backbone of the business.

The Birth of DRSS India

Over the years, the synergy of experience, innovation, and trust between both enterprises led to the creation of DRSS India, a unified vision that combines technical precision with modern operational excellence. Today, DRSS India continues to expand its domain, manufacturing high-quality cast iron weights and sheins, while offering calibration, rental, and industrial weighing solutions across India.
